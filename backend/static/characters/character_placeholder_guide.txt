# Phonics Tutor Character Image Guide

## Character Image Requirements

For best results with SadTalker, your character image should:

1. **Show the full face clearly**
   - Front-facing or slight angle
   - Both eyes visible
   - Mouth area clearly visible

2. **Have good lighting**
   - Even lighting on the face
   - No harsh shadows
   - Clear contrast

3. **Be high resolution**
   - Minimum 512x512 pixels
   - 1024x1024 or higher recommended
   - Clear, sharp image

4. **Have a simple background**
   - Solid color or simple gradient
   - No competing elements
   - Professional appearance

## Character Options

### Option 1: Free Stock Photo Sites
- Unsplash (https://unsplash.com)
- Pexels (https://pexels.com)
- Pixabay (https://pixabay.com)

Search terms: "cartoon teacher", "friendly character", "educational mascot"

### Option 2: AI Image Generation (Free)
- Bing Image Creator (https://www.bing.com/create)
- Canva (https://www.canva.com)

Prompt suggestions:
- "Friendly cartoon teacher for kids, simple background, front view, educational"
- "Cute animated tutor character, colorful, child-friendly design"
- "Professional cartoon educator, warm smile, approachable"

### Option 3: Draw/Create Yourself
- Use any drawing software
- Simple cartoon style works well
- Consistent appearance is key

## File Specifications

- **Format:** PNG or JPG
- **Filename:** tutor_reference.png
- **Location:** backend/static/characters/tutor_reference.png
- **Size:** 1024x1024 pixels recommended
- **Aspect Ratio:** 1:1 (square)

## Example Character Types That Work Well

1. **Cartoon Teacher**
   - Friendly appearance
   - Professional but approachable
   - Clear facial features

2. **Animated Mascot**
   - Fun and engaging
   - Kid-friendly design
   - Exaggerated expressions

3. **Simple Illustrated Character**
   - Clean lines
   - Bright colors
   - Clear mouth area

## What to Avoid

- ❌ Profile views (side of face)
- ❌ Sunglasses or face coverings
- ❌ Poor lighting
- ❌ Busy backgrounds
- ❌ Low resolution
- ❌ Extreme angles
- ❌ Multiple people in one image

## Temporary Placeholder

If you want to test quickly, you can use any clear portrait photo as a temporary placeholder. Just ensure:
- The person is looking directly at the camera
- The mouth area is clearly visible
- The lighting is good

Replace it with your final character design later.
